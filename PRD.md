# Product Requirements Document: Classy Cloud

## Problem
There isn't an easy way to classify clouds in a picture on your phone. For curious individuals look back on sky pictures and wonder, "what clouds are these?" their question
remain unanswered.

## Users
- Weather enthusiasts
- Smartphone users
- Nature-curious users 

## Goals
- MVP: Classify ~4 cloud types with >80% accuracy
- Intuitive and cute GUI for non-technical users

## User Story
As a user, I want to upload or drag a photo of the sky, so I can know what kind of clouds are in the image.

## Success Metrics
- < 2 sec inference time
- > 80% classification accuracy
